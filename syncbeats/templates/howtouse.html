How to use

Select no copyright YouTube video

Search in YouTube for track you want to play at your special moment. You only can use tracks with no copyrights.

Find the moment of drop

In this video find the exact moment of drop, chorus, or just the brightest part of the song. Paste link with specified start timeYouTube allows you to share link with specified start time. Click Share and check “Start: XX:XX” checkbox.

Set the time when you want drop to be played

Now you should set the time, when drop must kick in. For example, 0:00 1st January - to celebrate New Year.

Relax and wait

Everything is ready to make your special moment even more special. All you need - just wait for it!
